Our Story

With over 12 years of culinary experience, our head chef founded Fabian Bakery & Pizza to bring authentic Italian flavors and natural artisan bread to Holyhead. Every dough is handmade daily, using traditional recipes passed down through generations. We believe in real ingredients, passion, and creating a warm local place for families and friends.
